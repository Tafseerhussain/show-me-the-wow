How Does It Work

YOUR IDEA

If you have that great idea or prototype, please click the Submit button. There, you’ll answer a few questions, sign the Terms and Conditions, and send us a 1 minute video describing, showing or displaying your idea or prototype.

We will air clips of the videos along with Vince and his team’s responses to it and even our fans from the show will tell us how much they like it to help us gauge the audience response.

The one that gets the best likes in general will get to move up to the COMPETITION!

THE COMPETITION

The chosen ones will pack their bags and make that journey to Vince's secret studio! There you will compete against other entrepreneurs, while working with Vince and his team of experts to make it into a real sellable product.

If you can make it through the various steps, win over our audience and get some presales, then you move up to the LAUNCH!

THE LAUNCH

This is where we produce the commercial with Vince or one of his celebrity contacts! But then again, you never know, Vince may think you might be the perfect host for your product!

We’re going to have all our resources working with you to customize that perfect script, produce and launch that prefect commercial.

The top commercial that sells the most in our Launch phase will get a multi -million dollar distribution deal with Vince’s company! And if every product is profitable, everyone will get a deal!
